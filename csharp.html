JJTillCounter

During my time as a shift manager at Jimmy Johns, I decided to make a desktop application that would assist managers, including myself, in counting tills and tracking records about tills.

To code this program, I used WPF(XAML) for the front-end of the program, C# for the back end of the program, and XML to use as a database for the program.
